Tetris Gameplay

Classic Tetris

Gameplay Description:

In Classic Tetris multiple shapes of varying color drop from the top of the screen. The player is able to rotate and horizontally move the shapes as they fall to the bottom of the play area. The goal of the game is for the player to complete as many lines as possible with the falling shapes before reaching the top of the play area. In this game, the player advances to the next level after completing ten lines on a given level. As levels increase, the speed at which the blocks fall increase making the game more difficult as time progresses.

Tetris 99

Gameplay Description:

Tetris 99 mechanically plays very similar to classic tetris. The main difference is that 99 players are playing all at once. The goal objective of Tetris 99 is to be the last player standing. In this version of tetris clearing lines sends "garbage" blocks to opposing players making their play space smaller. This makes it so they reach the top of the screen faster, leading to them being eliminated from the game.
