Tetris Gameplay

Classic Tetris

Gameplay Description:

In Classic Tetris multiple shapes of varying color drop from the top of the screen. The player is able to rotate and horizontally move the shapes as they fall to the bottom of the play area. The goal of the game is for the player to complete as many lines as possible with the falling shapes before reaching the top of the play area. In this game, the player advances to the next level after completing ten lines on a given level. As levels increase, the speed at which the blocks fall increase making the game more difficult as time progresses.

Tetris 99

Gameplay Description:

Tetris 99 mechanically plays very similar to classic tetris. The main difference is that 99 players are playing all at once. The goal objective of Tetris 99 is to be the last player standing. In this version of tetris clearing lines sends "garbage" blocks to opposing players making their play space smaller. This makes it so they reach the top of the screen faster, leading to them being eliminated from the game.

Tetris Effect

Gameplay Description:

In Tetris Effect the majority of the gameplay mechanics are the same as the original Tetris. Tetris Effect is a visually more dynamic experience than the original Tetris as new animations have been created and each stage has its own unique graphics. The new twist Tetris Effect introduces is the "Zone". Clearing lines increases the Zone meter which a player is able to active. When the Zone is active gravity stops and finished lines move to the bottom of the play area. The Zone state ends when time runs out or when the player reaches the top of the play area. Once the Zone is finished all finished lines are cleared. The highest Zone clear is named "WTFtris", which requires the player to finish 25+ lines while in the Zone state.

Sand Tetris (SANDTRIS)

Gameplay Description:

Sand Tetris, also known as Sandtris is a game based off the original Tetris. Sandtris introduces one new gameplay element. Falling blocks no longer stay as blocks one they have been placed. Instead, the blocks become sand. To clear a line in Sandtris sand of the same color must reach from the left of the gameplay area, to the right of the gameplay area. This game requires the player to be careful when placing blocks to prevent unwanted sand colors from interfering with a potential line clear.
